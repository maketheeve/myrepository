Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on Aug 19th, 2025

Make The Eve believes in helping its customers as far as possible, and has therefore a liberal cancellation policy. Under this policy:

1) Cancellation Requests

  • Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made within 1–2 days of placing the order.
  • Requests may not be entertained if the order has been communicated to vendors/merchants and shipping has been initiated.

2) Non‑Cancellable Items

BnB does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers and eatables. However, refund/replacement can be made if the customer establishes that the quality of product delivered is not good.

3) Damaged or Defective Items

In case of receipt of damaged or defective items, please report the same to our Customer Service team. The request will be entertained once the merchant has checked and determined the same at their end. This should be reported within 1–2 days of receipt of the products.

4) Product Not as Described

If you feel that the product received is not as shown on the site or as per your expectations, you must bring it to the notice of our Customer Service within 1–2 days of receiving the product. The Customer Service Team, after reviewing your complaint, will take an appropriate decision.

5) Manufacturer Warranty

In case of complaints regarding products that come with a warranty from manufacturers, please refer the issue to them.

6) Refund Timelines

In case of any refunds approved by BnB, it will take 1–2 working days for the refund to be processed to the end customer. Actual credit timelines may depend on your bank or payment provider.
